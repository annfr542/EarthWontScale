Natural habitats purifies our water, protects us from floods, delivers pollinators to food crops to support nutritious diets, and provides countless other benefits to people. Use the map to explore what the unmet need for nature’s contributions was at 2015, and what they will look like in 2050 for three different future scenarios. The colours show the unmet need depending on what scenario you choose for our future.
